    About

    We would proudly like to introduce ourselves to you. We have been running this restaurant for the past 20 years and now and have gowned and improved with each passing day.

    We love to hear suggestions and improvement tips from you as it help us to grow & strengthens us. We would like to tell you about some of our intersting services.

    • Our waiters know their jobs, are efficient and able to advise customers on the best choices. Food is served with minimal delay, and after the meal patrons don’t have to struggle to get their bill.
    • The co-ordination between the kitchen and floor is a well-orchestrated ballet at a great restaurant and you'll never see your meal slowly congealing under a heat lamp at the pass, nor will your plates be whisked away and replaced by the next course while you're mid-chew. Rather the delivery of meals is well-spaced and consistent, with enough time for pleasant conversation and appetite whetting but not enough time to reach the dangerously "hangry" stage.
    • Global Village offer comfortable chairs that are an appropriate height and not so deep diners need to lean forward to reach the table. Our tables are decently sized so wait staff don't have to rearrange the water glasses with each course. They are set far enough apart so diners don't have to do that awkward rise and half turn to get past without putting their bottom in their neighbour's face or knocking over their chardonnay.

    Breakfast

    Start your day with a hearty breakfast at Global Village. From light and refreshing parfaits to the always popular Eggs Benedict, our menu offers something for everyone.

    Lunch & Weekend Brunch

    The seasonal Lunch menu at Global Village offers guests the perfect blend of unique, memorable dishes in a casual, relaxed atmosphere. Starters whet the appetite, Salads always boast fresh ingredients, and some exceptional mains, celebrating the cuisines of the world while still providing diners with something familiar. Main dishes serve to fill your empty stomach and satisfy your taste buds.

    We are now serving Weekend Brunch! Grab your friends and join us every Saturday and Sunday from 11am to 2pm. Reservations are recommended.

    Dinner

    The Dinner menu at Global Village features carefully curated Starters, flavorful Salads, and a Selection of extensive mains using a variety of Seasonal and local products of our chefs choosing.

    For the adventurous, our Six Course Tasting Menu offers a large selection of small plates, each featuring the highest quality ingredients. Add wine pairings to make it truly an experience to remember.

    Dessert

    No meal is complete without dessert. Enjoy one of our decadent creations. From chocolate to cheesecake, we have something for everyone. There is always room for dessert. Too full? Why not select from our extensive list of ports, brandy’s and cognac.

    Wine

    Our wide selection offers guests the unique opportunity to try new wines, while enhancing their overall knowledge about world wines. The wine list touches on all the major wine-growing regions of the world, including France, Italy, California, Australia, and New Zealand. Because this is a Downtown Edmonton boutique hotel, Canadian Wines are also represented, producing vintages that wonderfully complement our Food & Beverage team’s nuanced approach to cuisine.

    Customized menus and wine pairings can be created for your group booking or your next corporate or special event in both Madison’s and the Vintage Room.
